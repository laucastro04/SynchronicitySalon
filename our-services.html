Color Services

Full Foil: A comprehensive 3-4+ hr lightening session, including a single Gloss/Toner for an all-over dimensional effect.

Half Foil: A 2-3 hr lightening session, including a single Gloss/Toner for beautifully highlighted hair.

Partial Foil: A 1-2 hr lightening session with 10-15 foils, perfect for refreshing highlights between full or half sessions.

Single Process: A 1-1.5 hr service for base or all-over color deposit, adding depth and richness to your hair.

Platinum Roots: A 2-3 hr solid lightener retouch with a Single Gloss, ensuring your roots shine with brilliance.

Platinum All Over: A 3-6 hr session for a full head of solid lightener, achieving a stunning platinum look.

Balayage: Indulge in a 3-4 hr hand-painted lightening technique, creating a natural, sun-kissed effect.

Partial Balayage: A 2-3 hr hand-painting service for subtle, customized highlights

Reverse Balayage: Transform high-maintenance color into low-maintenance beauty with a 2-3 hr dimensional color deposit.
